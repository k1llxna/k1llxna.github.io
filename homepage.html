C++ Game Engine

Made with C++.

Engine is created in a OOP style.

Pixel Pandemonium: Game Design Document

A week long collaborative Game Jam, Pixel Pandemonium is a Plants vs Zombies inspired turn-based tower defense game where the player must defend their base from 4 directions! Both the player and the enemy have access to powering up their units with Candy Crush swiping mechanics. Players must strategize their defense among 4 lanes while being spatially aware of their defense layout!
